Common Questions

Are you insured?

Yes! We carry $1 million in general liability insurance through Spinnaker Insurance Company. Our policy covers property damage, bodily injury, and provides comprehensive protection for both you and us. We provide certificates of insurance to all clients for your records.

Are you background checked?

Yes. We completed a comprehensive professional background check in July 2025 through Checkr - the result is clear. We're happy to provide verification upon request. Your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities.

What can SafeMed do?

We organize medications per doctor's orders, clean medical equipment, coordinate pharmacy refills (with authorization), and relay requests to doctors. We do NOT provide medical care, nursing services, or medical advice. We work strictly from your physician's written orders.

What's your healthcare experience?

We have 5+ years of experience as a Dialysis Technician and Medical Office Coordinator. This includes hands-on patient care, medication administration, medical equipment operation, and healthcare coordination. We're CPR/BLS certified and trained in medication safety protocols.

How does pharmacy coordination work?

With written authorization, we coordinate refills with your pharmacy following your doctor's medication list. We handle administrative logistics only - tracking when refills are due, calling the pharmacy, and arranging pickup or delivery. No medical decisions are made.

Do you accept insurance?

Services are private-pay, which allows us to spend quality time without insurance restrictions. We provide detailed receipts that many families submit to HSA/FSA accounts for reimbursement. We'll provide whatever documentation you need.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Services are month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. No long-term contracts. If you're not satisfied after the first visit, we'll refund the unused portion - no questions asked.
