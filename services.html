Medication Management

We maintain an accurate medication list, prepare pillboxes (7‑day or 14‑day) with double‑checks, and coordinate refills before supplies run out. Families receive concise updates and photo proof every visit—so nothing slips through the cracks.

  • Reconciliation with provider orders
  • Pillbox prep with clear labels and safety double‑check
  • Refill coordination and escalation if issues arise
  • Visit notes + photos to the family

DME Cleaning & Care (All Devices Covered)

We sanitise all durable medical equipment in the home—CPAP systems, oxygen concentrators, nebulisers, walkers, wheelchairs, canes, commodes, and more—following manufacturer guidance to reduce infection risk and keep equipment dependable.

  • Full‑home DME cleaning each visit (no per‑item fees)
  • Manufacturer‑guided methods + safety checks
  • Photo proof and replacement recommendations
  • Monthly or bi‑weekly cadence
