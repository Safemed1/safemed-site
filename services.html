Comprehensive support for medication & DME

Weekly Medication Organization

We visit your home to fill pillboxes, reconcile medications with doctor orders, and ensure all prescriptions and supplements are up to date. Our organized approach prevents missed doses and medication errors.

  • Inventory review and reconciliation
  • Pillbox filling and labeling
  • Coordination with pharmacies and prescribers

Proactive Medication Reminders

Between visits, we provide call/text reminders tailored to your loved one's schedule. Our team monitors adherence and follows up promptly if doses are missed.

  • Daily reminder calls or texts
  • Custom schedule based on patient needs
  • Immediate follow‑up if a dose is delayed or missed

Monthly DME Sanitization

We clean and disinfect durable medical equipment—including CPAP masks and tubing, walkers, nebulizers, commodes, and more—using manufacturer‑recommended techniques to ensure safety and longevity.

  • CPAP and oxygen equipment
  • Walkers, canes, rollators, wheelchairs
  • Nebulizers and commodes

Family & Caregiver Updates

Transparency builds trust. After each visit, we send concise notes and photos to keep families informed. You’ll know exactly what was done and when.

  • Summary of tasks performed
  • Photos of organized medications or sanitized equipment
  • Secure digital log accessible to authorized family members

How we differ from traditional caregivers

Caregivers help with activities of daily living. SafeMed Concierge specializes in medication and equipment care. Our clinicians bridge the gap between the home and healthcare providers, offering:

  • Medication expertise: We understand dosing, drug interactions, and proper storage.
  • Clinical oversight: Our founder is experienced in clinical services coordination and dialysis treatment.
  • Detailed documentation: Visit notes and photos keep everyone informed.
  • Flexible, private-pay model: No insurance barriers—families get the support they need, when they need it.

Our process, step by step

From your first call to ongoing support, we follow a proven workflow to keep medications and equipment on track.

Intake & Onboarding

We gather health history, current medications and prescriber information. A clinician visits to meet you and set up your plan.

Weekly Pillbox Prep

We reconcile prescriptions, fill pillboxes and label doses. Any discrepancies are checked with your pharmacist or doctor.

Daily Reminders

Custom call or text reminders ensure doses aren’t missed. We follow up quickly if a dose is delayed.

Monthly Sanitization

We clean CPAP masks, walkers and nebulizers using manufacturer guidelines to maximize safety and life span.

Reporting & Updates

After each visit you receive notes and photos. We coordinate with your care team and adjust plans as needed.
