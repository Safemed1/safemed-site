About SafeMed Concierge

We focus on two essentials—medications and equipment—so you can focus on moments that matter.

Our Mission

To protect the health and comfort of seniors by managing medications with precision and keeping medical equipment clean and safe—so families can focus on time together.

Why We Started

We saw families trying to juggle complex medication schedules and device upkeep on top of everything else. Small mistakes can have big consequences. SafeMed Concierge was created to handle these essentials with consistency and care.

What Makes Us Different

Focused expertise

We specialise in medication management and DME cleaning only—nothing else.

Professional standards

Privacy‑minded, detail‑oriented and reliable. We take your trust seriously.

Clear communication

Brief updates after visits and collaborative decisions with family keep everyone aligned.

Dignity & respect

We work at the client’s pace, in their home, with patience and compassion.

Our Promise

Accuracy, safety and steady support—delivered with kindness.

Our Founder

Joseph Duldulao is a clinical services coordinator and nursing professional who founded SafeMed Concierge to bring dignity and consistency to seniors' medication routines. His experience across home healthcare, treatment centres and dialysis clinics equips him with the expertise to oversee medication management and equipment sanitisation with compassion and precision.

Joseph is currently pursuing a nursing degree and holds certifications in CPR, BLS and hemodialysis. He speaks Ilocano, Tagalog and conversational Spanish, enabling clear communication with diverse families.
