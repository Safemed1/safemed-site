Joseph Duldulao, Founder & CEO

Joseph Duldulao is a clinical services coordinator and nursing professional who founded SafeMed Concierge to bring dignity and consistency to seniors' medication routines. His diverse experience across home healthcare, treatment centers, and dialysis clinics equips him with the expertise to oversee medication management and equipment sanitization with compassion and precision.

  • Clinical expertise: Coordinated care across clinicians, patients, and doctors, scheduled mobile visits and lab draws, and performed chart audits to ensure HIPAA-compliant documentation.
  • Patient care background: Served as an overnight clinical assistant, monitoring patients every 15 minutes, administering medications as ordered, and providing mentorship to team members.
  • Dialysis & equipment proficiency: Administered dialysis treatments and trained new staff at a kidney care clinic, ensuring safe operation and patient comfort.
  • Education & certifications: Currently pursuing a nursing degree, with training in nursing assistant, phlebotomy, and behavioral therapy; certified in CPR, BLS and hemodialysis.
  • Languages: Fluent in Ilocano and Tagalog, with advanced Spanish, enabling clear communication with diverse families.

Our Mission

We believe every older adult deserves independence, dignity, and reliable support. SafeMed Concierge is dedicated to:

  • Keeping medications organized and doses on schedule.
  • Ensuring durable medical equipment is sanitized and safe.
  • Providing clear, timely updates to families and caregivers.
  • Coordinating with doctors, pharmacists and home health teams to streamline care.
  • Upholding HIPAA standards and protecting personal health information at every step.

Why SafeMed?

Typical caregivers focus on daily living assistance. We focus on medication safety and equipment health. Here’s how we stand apart:

  • Clinical oversight: Services are designed by a healthcare professional who understands physician orders and documentation.
  • Medication-focused: Our visits prioritize pillbox organization, medication reconciliation, and reminders instead of general caregiving tasks.
  • Equipment care: We sanitize CPAP equipment, walkers, nebulizers, and other devices according to manufacturer guidelines.
  • Transparent updates: Each visit includes notes and photos that you can review, ensuring accountability and peace of mind.
